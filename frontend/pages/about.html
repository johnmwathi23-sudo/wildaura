Our Story

How It Began

A Love Letter to African Beauty

Wild Aura was born in Nairobi, Kenya — a city of vibrant energy, warm sunsets, and women who carry themselves with an unapologetic glow. Our founder, Wanjiku Mwangi, grew up watching her grandmother mix turmeric, honey, and aloe vera in the family kitchen, creating remedies that kept three generations of women radiant. When Wanjiku moved abroad for her studies, she searched high-end department stores for skincare that reminded her of home. She found luxury. She found science. But she never found the warmth, the intentionality, the heart of what her grandmother made with her hands.

So she created it. Wild Aura is the bridge between ancestral African beauty wisdom and modern dermatological science. Every bottle carries the memory of her grandmother's hands, the golden dust of turmeric on a wooden counter, and the belief that the best skincare does not come from a laboratory alone — it comes from the earth, from tradition, from women who knew the secrets of nature long before they were called "formulas."

Today, Wild Aura serves thousands of women across Africa and the diaspora. We are not just a skincare brand. We are a homecoming.

What We Stand For

Our Mission & Values

Three pillars that guide every decision, every ingredient, every bottle we create.

🌿

Clean Beauty

We believe luxury should never come at the cost of your health. Every Wild Aura product is formulated without parabens, sulfates, silicones, synthetic fragrances, or mineral oil. Our ingredients are meticulously sourced from ethical suppliers, dermatologist tested, and formulated to work in harmony with your skin's natural biology. Clean does not mean weak. It means intentional. It means effective. It means safe enough for your most vulnerable days and powerful enough for your most radiant ones.

🌍

African Heritage

African women have been practicing advanced skincare for centuries. Turmeric for brightening. Aloe for healing. Shea for deep moisture. These are not trends to us — they are inheritance. Wild Aura honors these traditions by collaborating with local farmers, sourcing ingredients from across the continent, and celebrating the wisdom of African women who have always known the power of botanical beauty. Every purchase is a tribute to the grandmothers, mothers, and sisters who kept the knowledge alive.

💚

Community Impact

Beauty should be a rising tide that lifts all women. Wild Aura is committed to empowering the communities behind our ingredients. We partner with women-led farming cooperatives in Kenya, Ghana, and Tanzania, ensuring fair wages, safe working conditions, and investment back into local communities. A percentage of every sale funds skincare education and entrepreneurship programs for young African women. When you glow with Wild Aura, you help another woman find her glow too.

Meet the Founder

Wanjiku Mwangi

Founder & CEO, Wild Aura

"My grandmother never bought a single skincare product in her life. She did not need to. She understood that beauty comes from the earth, from intention, from ritual. Wild Aura is my way of taking what she knew and giving it to the world — in a bottle that reflects the luxury we deserve. I want every woman who uses Wild Aura to feel what I felt watching my grandmother: that she is enough. That her skin is a gift. That her glow is her birthright."

Wanjiku holds a degree in Biochemistry from the University of Nairobi and an MBA from the London School of Economics. She spent a decade in the global beauty industry before returning to Kenya to build Wild Aura from a small kitchen in Lavington. Today, her team spans Nairobi, Accra, and London — but every formula is still made with the same care her grandmother taught her. Wanjiku lives in Nairobi with her daughter, Zuri, who she hopes will inherit not just a business, but a legacy of African excellence.
